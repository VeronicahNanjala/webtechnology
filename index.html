Listen to African music anywhere anytime

Ngoma is ‘Music’ in an African dialect and it symbolises the rhythms, instruments, concoctions and heart of authentic African music. With your new Ngoma software, we provide you with variety of African music styles regardless of its dialects. We never want you to loose your roots wherever you go. Thus, connect to home with Ngoma because you won’t miss a thing in the Music industry.
